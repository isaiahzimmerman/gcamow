Grove City Area Meals on Wheels is a neighborhood service that delivers fresh, nutritious, and well-balanced meals directly to your home at a low cost.

We prepare and provide hot, nutritious meals 5 days a week to the aged, convalescent and handicapped individuals of our community allowing them to live independently in their own home.

Delivery locations include parts of Grove City, Leesburg, Mercer, Harrisville & Slippery Rock.

Meals

Our meals are delivered 5 days a week (except major holidays). They are delivered by volunteers to your home Monday through Friday between 11 a.m. & Noon.

Each meal is well-balanced with healthy portions—We deliver on a five-week rotation consisting of a variety of dietician-approved meals. Milk, bread & butter are also included with the hot meal.

A hot meal costs $4.00 per day, or $6.00 per day if you choose a hot meal plus a light cold supper.

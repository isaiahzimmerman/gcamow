Grove City Area Meals on Wheels is a neighborhood service that delivers fresh, nutritious, and well-balanced meals directly to your home at a low cost.

We prepare and provide hot, nutritious meals 5 days a week to persons who are unable to provide for themselves, allowing them to live independently in their own home.

Delivery locations include areas of Grove City, Leesburg, Mercer, Harrisville & Slippery Rock.

Meals

Our meals are well balanced with healthy portions and have been approved by a dietician. There is a nice variety of meals since we use a 5-week menu rotation. Our volunteers deliver the meals to your home, Monday-Friday, except major holidays, between 11 AM & Noon. Our holidays include New Years Day, Memorial Day, 4th of July, Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day, Black Friday, and Christmas Day.

The cost of a hot meal is $ a day which would consist of a protein, vegetable, potato, fruit, bread & butter and milk.

For $ a day, you get a hot meal and a cold light supper. A sample cold meal includes a sandwich, salad and juice.

In the event that we would have to close due to severe weather conditions etc., notices will be posted on Facebook, on this website and on television under Closings on Youngstown channels WFMJ (21), WKBN (27) and WYTV (33) and on Pittsburgh channels KDKA (2) and WPXI (11).
